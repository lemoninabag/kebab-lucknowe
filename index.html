About Us

Your taste in the heart of Ajman

Welcome to Kebab Lucknowe

Variety of flavours under one roof

At Kebab Lucknowe, we bring the authentic taste of Lucknow to Ajman, UAE. We specialize in traditional Lucknowe kebabs, aromatic biryani, and crispy broasted chicken.

Located in the heart of Ajman, we serve the local community with fresh, high-quality ingredients and traditional cooking methods. From our signature Lucknowe Special to our perfectly spiced biryani, each dish is prepared with authentic flavors that transport you to the streets of Lucknow.

Whether you're craving traditional Lucknowe kebab, or looking for the best biryani in Ajman, Kebab Lucknowe is your destination for an unforgettable culinary journey in the UAE.
